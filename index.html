About

GBDA student, president‑level student leader, and coach who’s used storytelling, prototyping, and experimentation to ship experiences for real audiences. Now focusing that same energy on AI‑enabled learning tools and collaborative workshop environments.

Background

Blends design, business, and technology through the Global Business and Digital Arts program at the University of Waterloo, with experience leading student initiatives and working on UX design jams with industry partners.

Outside of coursework, applies coaching and communication skills from sport to design teams: breaking down complex skills, setting clear goals, and giving actionable feedback.

How AI fits in

Uses AI tools as thinking partners for research synthesis, idea generation, and quick prototyping—while staying critical about limitations, bias, and failure modes.

Especially interested in how AI can support reflection, metacognition, and personalized learning journeys, aligned with Futures Lab’s focus on “Learning Re‑Imagined”.
